MITK CAMPUS FACILITIES AND INFRASTRUCTURE

Laboratories:
Modern computer labs, electronics labs, civil labs, mechanical workshops

Hostels:
Separate boys and girls hostels with dining and Wi-Fi

Library:
Digital library, e-journals, physical books, reading rooms

Cafeteria:
Hygienic food with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options

Sports:
Indoor and outdoor sports facilities

Medical:
On-campus medical assistance

Transport:
College buses from various routes in Udupi and Kundapura

Incubation Center:
Technology Business Incubator (TBI) for startups

MITK provides comprehensive facilities for student learning, accommodation, recreation, and entrepreneurship development.
